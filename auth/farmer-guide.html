üöÄ Getting Started

Welcome to EcoSterile! This guide will help you understand and use your pH monitoring system from the beginning.

What is EcoSterile?

EcoSterile is a smart system that automatically controls the pH level in your farm's water. It measures the acidity/alkalinity of water and adds chemicals automatically to keep it at the perfect level for your crops.

üìå Important

The pH measurements come from the EcoSterile Device (sensor) that you connect to your mobile through internet. Make sure your device is properly installed and connected.

üîß Device Setup

1
Get Your EcoSterile Device

You will receive an EcoSterile Device (sensor box) that measures the pH of water.

2
Connect the Sensor to Water

Place the sensor probe into your water tank or irrigation system. Make sure it is fully submerged and properly secured.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning

Do NOT drop the sensor or apply excessive force. Handle it carefully to avoid damage.

3
Connect Device to WiFi/Internet

The device needs internet connection to send data to your mobile. Follow the device instructions to connect it to your WiFi network.

4
Calibrate the Sensor (First Time Only)

When first installed, calibrate your sensor using calibration solutions as per the device manual. This ensures accurate readings.

‚öôÔ∏è How Automatic Pump Control Works

Once you set your crop, the system automatically controls the pump:

  1. Measures pH: Every few seconds, the sensor reads the water pH
  2. Compares with Optimal Range: Checks if pH is within the good range for your crop
  3. Activates Pump if Needed:
    • If pH is TOO HIGH ‚Üí Activates ACIDIC pump (to lower pH)
    • If pH is TOO LOW ‚Üí Activates BASIC pump (to raise pH)
    • If pH is GOOD ‚Üí Does nothing
  4. Logs Everything: Records each pump activation with time and amount
üìå Important

The system will only activate pumps if the pH is significantly outside the ideal range. This prevents over-correction and saves chemicals.

üìÖ Daily Operations (What You Need to Do)

Every Morning:

Open the EcoSterile app on your mobile
Check if the current pH is GREEN (good) or RED (problem)
Look at the pump log to see what happened overnight

Every 2-3 Days:

Manually check the water pH with a test kit (optional but recommended)
Compare with what the app shows to verify accuracy

Every Week:

Check the 7-day pH trend graph
Make sure chemical tanks are not empty
Check that the device is still connected and working

Every Month:

Clean the sensor probe gently with distilled water
Check if sensor needs recalibration
Review the full month's data and pump logs

üîß Troubleshooting

Problem: Device Not Connected

‚ùå
Solution
  • Check if WiFi is working
  • Restart the device
  • Check internet connection is available
  • Move closer to the WiFi router

Problem: pH Reading Seems Wrong

‚ùå
Solution
  • Make sure sensor is fully submerged in water
  • Sensor might need cleaning (use distilled water)
  • Sensor might need recalibration
  • Verify manually with a pH test kit

Problem: Pump Not Activating

‚ùå
Solution
  • Check if chemical tanks have liquid
  • Check if pump power is on
  • pH might already be in optimal range
  • Contact support if problem persists

‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Tips

Important Safety Guidelines
  • Always wear gloves when handling chemicals
  • Keep pH chemicals away from children and animals
  • Never mix different chemicals together
  • Follow chemical storage instructions carefully
  • If chemicals spill on skin, wash immediately with water
  • Never taste or drink the water from the system
  • Turn off the system before maintenance or cleaning

üí° Tips for Best Results

Pro Tips
  • Keep chemicals fresh: Replace chemicals regularly to maintain effectiveness
  • Monitor trends: Look at weekly trends, not just daily readings
  • Water quality: Cleaner water needs fewer adjustments
  • Maintenance: Regular sensor cleaning prevents errors
  • Records: Keep crop records to learn what works best
  • Ask questions: Use the in-app chatbot assistant for help anytime
